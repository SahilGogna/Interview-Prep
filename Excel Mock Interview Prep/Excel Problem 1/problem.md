# Excel Problem 1: Product Sales Analysis

**Difficulty:** Easy

## Context

REC Corp LTD. is a small-scale business venture established in India. They have been selling **four products (P1, P2, P3, P4)** for over ten years.

They have collected data from their retail centers and organized it into a CSV file.

**Dataset**: [data.csv](https://github.com/SahilGogna/Interview-Prep/blob/main/Excel%20Mock%20Interview%20Prep/Excel%20Problem%201/data.csv)

---

## Dataset Information

The file contains 8 numerical parameters:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4 | Total unit sales of products 1-4 |
| S1, S2, S3, S4 | Total revenue from products 1-4 |

**Example:** On 13-06-2010, product 1 was bought by 5,422 people and generated INR 17,187.74 in revenue.

---

## Tasks

Import the data in Excel and answer these questions:

1. Is there any **trend in sales** of all four products during certain months?

2. Out of all four products, which product has seen the **highest sales** in all the given years?

3. The company has all its retail centers closed on December 31st every year. Estimate the **number of units of each product** that could be sold on Dec 31st if all retail centers were kept open.

4. The CEO is considering **dropping production of one product**. Analyze the data and suggest whether this would result in a massive setback for the company.

5. **Predict sales and revenues for 2024** with the best possible accuracy.
