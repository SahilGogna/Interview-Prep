# Excel Problem 2: Retail Sales Analysis

**Difficulty:** Medium

## Context

RetailVerse Pvt. Ltd. is a growing retail venture operating across several cities, offering a wide range of consumer products.

They have shared a dataset containing sales and customer demographic details collected from their retail stores.

**Dataset**: [data.csv](https://github.com/SahilGogna/Interview-Prep/blob/main/Excel%20Mock%20Interview%20Prep/Excel%20Problem%202/data.csv)

---

## Dataset Information

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Transaction ID | Unique identifier for each transaction |
| Date | Date of the transaction |
| Customer ID | Unique identifier for each customer |
| Gender | Gender of the customer |
| Age | Age of the customer |
| Product Category | Category of the product purchased |
| Quantity | Number of units purchased |
| Price per Unit | Price of one item |
| Total Amount | Total sale value (Quantity Ã— Price per Unit) |

---

## Tasks

Import the dataset into Excel and answer these questions:

1. How does customer **age** and **gender** influence their purchasing behavior?

2. Are there any noticeable **sales trends** across different months or seasons?

3. Which **product categories** generate the highest total revenue and have the highest number of transactions?

4. What is the **average spending** across different age groups and genders?

5. How does the **quantity purchased per transaction** vary among product categories?

6. Are there any **seasonal patterns** in customer purchasing habits?

7. What insights can be derived from the **price and total amount distribution** across categories?
