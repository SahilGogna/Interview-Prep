# Excel Problem 3: Boat Sales Analysis

**Difficulty:** Medium

## Context

You are working as a data analyst for a yacht and boat sales website. The marketing team is preparing a weekly newsletter for boat owners.

The newsletter is designed to help sellers get more views of their boat, as well as stay on top of market trends.

**Dataset**: [data.csv](https://github.com/SahilGogna/Interview-Prep/blob/main/Excel%20Mock%20Interview%20Prep/Excel%20Problem%203/data.csv)

---

## Tasks

Import the dataset into Excel and analyze:

1. What are the **characteristics of the most viewed boat listings** in the last 7 days?

2. Is it the **most expensive boats** that get the most views?

3. Are there **common features** among the most viewed boats?

4. What insights can you provide to help sellers **improve their listing visibility**?
