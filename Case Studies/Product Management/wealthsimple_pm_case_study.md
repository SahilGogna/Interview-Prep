# Solve It Together — PM Case Study
## Wealthsimple | Referral Program Metrics Review

You are a **Product Manager at Wealthsimple**, Canada's leading fintech platform. The company recently launched a referral program to accelerate sign-up growth. You are in your weekly metrics review and you notice something concerning. Your VP asks you to walk the team through what is happening and what to do about it.

---

## Dataset: Key Metrics — Last 30 Days vs Previous 30 Days

| Metric | Previous 30 Days | Last 30 Days | Change |
|--------|-----------------|--------------|--------|
| New Sign-Ups | 5,000 | 6,000 | +20% |
| 30-Day Retention | 40% | 28% | -12pp |
| Activation Rate (first deposit made) | 55% | 42% | -13pp |
| Avg Time to First Deposit | 3 days | 6 days | +3 days |
| Users from Referral Program | 15% | 45% | +30pp |
| Users from Organic Search | 50% | 28% | -22pp |
| Users from Paid Ads | 35% | 27% | -8pp |

---
